title: My experience

I'm the Lead Product Manager at [Acorn Intelligence](https://acorn-i.com/), an eCommerce and
advertising agency helping brands grow across Amazon, Walmart, and direct-to-consumer channels.

I own [Ignite](https://ignite.acorn-i.com/), our proprietary analytics and workflow platform used
by 250+ brands, 15+ agencies, and 1,200 users. It processes millions of rows of retailer, media,
inventory, and first-party shopper data daily, and I took it from an internal tool to a fully
licensed SaaS product.

I also took Texana from proof-of-concept to product: an agentic GenAI solution embedded in Ignite
that gives eCommerce and Retail Media teams a collaborative ecosystem of specialised AI agents for
analytics, modelling, and reporting. It cut a three-week post-Prime Day reporting project to three
days when repeated for Spring Deals, and is driving upsell conversations with existing customers.

Previously, I was a Senior Product Manager at
[The Exchange Lab](https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-exchange-lab/about/), a
[WPP](https://www.wpp.com/) company whose programmatic advertising platform,
[Proteus](https://www.wpp.com/news/2015/12/groupm-acquires-programmatic-marketing-solutions-company-the-exchange-lab),
was acquired by GroupM. Earlier in my career, I was a Product Manager at [Ask.com](https://uk.ask.com),
working on large-scale, data-driven products in search and content.
