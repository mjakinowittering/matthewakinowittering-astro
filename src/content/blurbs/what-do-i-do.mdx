title: What I do
---

I build data products that give people clarity, whether that's a real-time analytics platform, an
AI agent that replaces a manual reporting workflow, or a bid optimisation algorithm that measurably
improves campaign performance.

I work closely with business leaders, customers, and engineering teams to find the opportunities
worth building, then see them through from idea to launch and beyond. I'm most at home on 0→1
products where the problem is real, the data is messy, and the value of getting it right is clear.
