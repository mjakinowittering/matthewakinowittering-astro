The most expensive mistakes in product development happen before a single feature is built. I work
cross-functionally to surface the four risks that sink products early: desirability, usability,
feasibility, and viability. Talking to real customers and stress-testing assumptions is how I make
sure we're solving the right problem, not just a plausible one.
