Once a team is aligned on what to build and why, my job is to protect that intent through delivery.
I work closely with designers, engineers, and stakeholders to keep decisions anchored to our goals,
and I'm willing to have the difficult conversation when scope creep threatens the work we've
committed to. Saying no clearly and honestly is part of what good product leadership looks like.
